🌅 Daily Walking & Hydration Plan

Morning Session

  • ☀️ 5:00 AM: Wake up and freshen up
  • 💧 5:10 AM: Drink 200–250 ml warm water
  • 👟 5:30–6:00 AM: Morning walk (30 mins). Optional sips (~50–100 ml every 15–20 mins)
  • 💧 6:00 AM: Finish walk and drink 250–300 ml warm water

Daytime Hydration

  • 💧 Maintain hydration every 1–2 hours (~150–250 ml), roughly 1.5–2 L across the day

Evening Session

  • 🌇 8:00–8:30 PM: Evening walk (30 mins). Optional sips (~50–100 ml every 15–20 mins)
  • 💧 8:30 PM: Finish walk and drink 250–300 ml warm water

Bedtime

  • 🌙 9–10 PM: Optional 100–150 ml warm water before bed

Tips for Best Results

  • ✅ Listen to thirst cues, especially in hot or humid conditions
  • ✅ Maintain regular sips throughout the day
  • ✅ Adjust slightly based on activity and climate
